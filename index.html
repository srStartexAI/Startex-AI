How the solution works with Google Cloud

Startex AI automates M&A by orchestrating scouting, valuation, matchmaking, and closing inside a single marketplace. Built on Google Cloud, the platform supports secure data ingestion, financial document processing, AI classification, real-time matching, and workflow automation for buyers, sellers, and certified M&A professionals.

The proprietary DealSeek algorithm runs on Vertex AI, analyzing financial and qualitative data to score opportunities and suggest the most relevant counterparties. BigQuery and Cloud Storage power the analytics layer, enabling secure, high-performance queries and pipelines across structured and unstructured deal data.

Cloud Run and Cloud Functions provide a serverless backbone for mandate intake, automated evaluations, document exchange, partner assignment, and transaction lifecycle management, allowing the team to focus on product logic instead of infrastructure operations.

Security & collaboration

The environment is secured with IAM, VPC Service Controls, and encryption in transit and at rest. Access policies are enforced across roles and organizations, protecting sensitive M&A data at every step.

Integration with Google Workspace lets advisors, buyers, and sellers collaborate on due-diligence documents, track negotiations, and close deals in a structured, auditable flow that remains fully aligned with corporate compliance requirements.
